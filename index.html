Das Konzept

The Concept

Build Skills.
Boost Flow.

Build Skills.
Boost Flow.

Das ThriveZone Retreat ist kein klassisches Fitness-Camp. Es ist eine bewusst designte Umgebung, die Verhalten verändert. In 5 Tagen erlebst du, wie Training, Ernährung, Mindset und Gewohnheiten zu einem integrierten System werden — das du danach mit nach Hause nimmst.

The ThriveZone Retreat is not a classic fitness camp. It's a deliberately designed environment that changes behaviour. Over 5 days you experience how training, nutrition, mindset and habits merge into one integrated system — one you take home with you.

💪

Training

Training

Kein isoliertes Gym-Training. Du tauchst ein in Ausdauer, Kraft, Mobilität und körperliche Flow-Aktivitäten — und lernst, wie du all das optimal in deinen Alltag integrierst. Deep Dives in Mobility, Bodyweight & Core Training und High Intensity Intervals. Morgens Laufen in Mallorcas Natur, abends funktionelles Krafttraining in der Gruppe. Mit Physio-Support und Bewegungsanalyse von Asrar.

No isolated gym sessions. You immerse yourself in endurance, strength, mobility and physical flow activities — and learn how to integrate all of it optimally into your everyday life. Deep dives in mobility, bodyweight & core training, and high intensity intervals. Morning runs through Mallorca's nature, evening functional strength training as a group. With physio support and movement analysis from Asrar.

Nutrition

Immersive learning, not theory. You cook ThriveZone recipes side by side — simple, specifically designed to strengthen the body and optimise micronutrients. Breakfast, lunch, dinner — all fresh, all intentional. And the best part: you sit together in a kitchen with views across the valley, and the conversation around the table is often the highlight of the day.

Meditation isn't a bonus — it's a structural part of the retreat. Alicja leads guided meditations every evening, shaping the transition from activation to deep sleep. You'll learn how breathwork regulates the nervous system, how stillness orders the mind, and how to reach flow not only in training — but in life. Circadian rhythm, light exposure and reflection complete the system.

Alicja — Meditations- & Achtsamkeits-Coach
Alicja — Meditation & Mindfulness Coach

Warum Meditation hier keine Nebensache ist: Sportliche Aktivierung erhöht Cortisol und BDNF — das Gehirn ist offen und formbar. Tiefe Regeneration verankert das Erlebte. Aber erst die Meditation macht den Kreis vollständig. Regelmäßige Meditationspraxis reduziert nachweislich chronischen Stress, verbessert die Schlafqualität, schärft die Fokus­fähigkeit und aktiviert das parasympathische Nervensystem — den Gegenpol zur dauerhaften Anspannung des modernen Alltags. Alicjas geführte Abend-Meditationen sind kein Esoterik-Programm. Sie sind ein präzises Werkzeug: Körper herunterregulieren, Gedanken ordnen, Tiefschlaf vorbereiten.

Why meditation is not an afterthought here: Athletic activation raises cortisol and BDNF — the brain becomes open and malleable. Deep regeneration anchors the experience. But meditation completes the loop. Regular meditation practice measurably reduces chronic stress, improves sleep quality, sharpens focus and activates the parasympathetic nervous system — the counterpart to the constant tension of modern life. Alicja's guided evening meditations are not a mystical programme. They are a precise tool: down-regulate the body, organise thoughts, prepare for deep sleep.
